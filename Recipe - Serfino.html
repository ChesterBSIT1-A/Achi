Mcdollibee Chimken Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Breading Mix
    •

    Methods on How to Cook:

    1. First add oil into the hot Frying Pan
    2. Second is coat the Chicken with Breading Mix
    3. Third put the coated Chicken into hot frying pan
    4. Cook 'til Golden Brown
    5. Serve and Enjoy
